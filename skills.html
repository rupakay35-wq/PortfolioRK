Operating Method

From partner agreement to partner productivity.

The process is intentionally simple: recruit for fit, enable around outcomes, create shared pipeline, measure progress and invest where results are repeatable.

Recruit

Prioritize capability, customer overlap and executive commitment.

Enable

Align messaging, use cases, technical readiness and incentives.

Co-Sell

Map accounts, share ownership and move opportunities forward.

Measure

Track pipeline, velocity, forecast accuracy, wins and gaps.

Scale

Deepen investment in productive partners and repeat what works.

My Story

I learned the channel from both sides of the table.

Nobody starts with a partner ecosystem—you build one relationship, one solution and one win at a time. I spent the first 12 years of my career in business development, building companies, selling SaaS and CaaS, opening enterprise doors and learning how executives actually buy.

When I moved into MSP/MSSP and cybersecurity, I brought that hunter mindset into the channel. Today, I connect VARs, SIs, MSPs, MSSPs and technology vendors around shared customers, practical joint GTM plans and measurable revenue.

My rule is simple: a partner agreement is only valuable when it creates enablement, pipeline, revenue and a customer who is better off. I like ambitious plans, clean CRM data, honest QBRs and relationships built to last longer than the deal.
